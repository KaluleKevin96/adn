Who We Are

ADN drilling Solutions Limited is a subsidiary of ADN Drilling and Mining, Auto Spares, and Transportation Limited Founded in the United Republic of Tanzania. ADN Tanzania was registered in Arusha Tanzania in 2013 with the goal of providing Drilling and mining, transportation, and water drilling services in the country and in the whole African region.

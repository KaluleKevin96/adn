About ADN

Introduction to ADN

ADN drilling Solutions Limited is a subsidiary of AON Drilling and Mining, Auto Spares, and Transportation Limited Founded in the United Republic of Tanzania. AON Tanzania was registered in Arusha Tanzania in 2013 with the goal of providing Drilling and mining, transportation, and water drilling services in the country and in the whole African region. With over six (6) years of experience in the sectors, ADN Drilling Solutions Africa Limited boasts of providing high quality services with a blend of a professional and qualified team which provides quality services to her clients. The team has lean but highly qualified staff ranging from Safety Reps, Drillers, Engineers, Technicians, and Project Managers.

ADN has over 6 years in the industry (drilling, mining and exploration]. ADNS management team leads by example, actively providing practical field assistance, technical advice and professional management to its personnel. We place intense focus on fostering the professional development of all AON Drilling staff members by providing employees with Safety on-going skills training, education and other work opportunities for personnel and company advancement

The management has substantial experience in establishing operations in emerging markets and complying with the country's requirements regarding local business laws & regulations, taxation, exchange control regulations, labour laws, goods import and customs controls.
